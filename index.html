Yamaha About Yamaha

Founded in 1955, Yamaha began its journey with a 125cc two-cycle, single cylinder motorcycle and after that there was no turning back for this Japanese manufacturer. With presence in almost every major automobile market, Yamaha has a significant line-up in the Philippines as well. The wide range of offering from Yamaha include Tricity placed in the Leaning Multi-Wheel Segment, which is one of its kind in the segment. The other models are NMax, Mio Soul i125, Mio 125, Mio Sporty, MXi in the Automatic segment. While the moped segment of the brand include Sniper 150 MXI, VegF1 and Vega Force i. In the street segment, the company has TFX, FZi, SZ, R3 and R15. STX125, RS110F are in Business segment and XTZ 125 and Serow 250 in the off-road segment.

Honda About Honda

It’s been several decades that Honda is producing class leading bikes and scooters. Not only in the Philippines but, across the globe Honda has established its roots and gained trust of the customers. In the Philippines the line-up of Honda include Click 125i, Zoomer-X, BeAT FI in the scooter segment. The sport segment has names like All-new CBR150R, CB150R Streetfire, CRF250L, XR150L and CBR150R. The underbone segment, which is one of the most likeable segment in the Philippines have RS150 Special Repsol Edition, RS150, RS 125, XRM 125 Motard and XRM 125 DSX. The business segment of Honda include TMX Supremo, TMS 125 Alpha and CB 125CL. Apart from cars and bikes, the next milestone that Honda has achieved is the Honda Jet Deliveries, which might begin in the Philippines as well.

Suzuki About Suzuki

The 9th largest automaker of the world, Suzuki has a vast experience of 107 years and with an extensive line-up in the Philippines, Suzuki is one of the leading bike manufacturers in the country. The underbone segment has Raider R 150, Raider J115 FI, Shooter 115 Fi, and Smash 115. In backbone segment, Suzuki offers Gixxer, AX4, and GD110. Address and Skydrive 125 are in scooter segment while big bikes include Hayabusa, RM-Z250, GSX-R750, GSX-S1000 ABS, V-Storm 1000 ABS, V-Storm 650 ABS, GSX-R 600 and GSR 750.
